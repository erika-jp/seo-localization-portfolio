Sample 01 — Meta Description
Meta Description
Product Page · Moisturizing Serum
160 chars · JP
English Original
Discover our best-selling hydrating serum, clinically tested for sensitive skin. Lightweight formula with hyaluronic acid. Free shipping on orders over $40.
Japanese Localization
敏感肌に特化した、皮膚科テスト済みの高保湿美容液。ヒアルロン酸配合の軽いつけ心地で、肌に自然になじみます。5,000円以上のご注文で送料無料。
Localization note: "$40" converted to the Japanese equivalent threshold (¥5,000) for local relevance. "Best-selling" removed as it can feel unnatural in Japanese SEO context; replaced with skin-benefit framing which performs better in Japanese search intent. Character count optimized for Google JP display (160 chars).
Sample 02 — Title Tag
Title Tag
Category Page · Suncare Line
60 chars · JP
English Original
Natural Sunscreen & Sun Care Products | SHIRO Skincare Official
Japanese Localization
日焼け止め・UVケアアイテム一覧｜SHIROスキンケア公式
Localization note: "Natural" omitted — in Japanese SEO, ingredient claims in titles require compliance caution (薬機法). "Sun Care" split into 日焼け止め and UVケア, which are the two dominant Japanese search terms for this category. Used Japanese pipe character ｜ (fullwidth) for visual consistency in search results.
